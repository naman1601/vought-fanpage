We are Vought.

We are a team of gifted individuals, known commonly around the world as "superheroes".

We are on a mission to make the world a better and safer place!

Our main Heroes.

Homelander. The leader of The Seven, Homelander is the strongest superhero in the world.

His powers include flight, super-strength, laser-vision and durability.

As a warrior, feminist, humanitarian, and the second most powerful member of The Seven, Queen Maeve is the ultimate role model for little girls all over the world.

Her powers include superhuman strength, speed, and durability. Maeve is an expert at combat.

Annie January is as down-to-earth and sincere as they come; the girl next door with superpowers.

Her powers include electrical conversion and energy attacks. She also has enhanced recovery.

Stormfront's public persona as an edgy, trendy and virtuous hero is a facade for her recklessness, racism and sadistic tendencies, which rival that of even Homelander.

Her powers include plasma manipulation and electrical telekinesis.
